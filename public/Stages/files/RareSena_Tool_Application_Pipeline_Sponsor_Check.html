Application Pipeline Tracker — with Sponsor Licence Check
Never again interview brilliantly for a job that could never sponsor you
The problem this solves: The most demoralising experience in immigrant job searching is the great interview followed by "unfortunately, we are not able to sponsor visas." The UK government publishes the Register of Licensed Sponsors — every organisation legally able to sponsor a Skilled Worker visa. Check the employer before you apply. If they are not on the register, they cannot sponsor you — no matter what the recruiter says.
Sponsor licence check
🔍 Check an employer before you apply
Type the exact company name (as registered at Companies House where possible). The app searches the official gov.uk Register of Licensed Sponsors (Worker).
Prototype note: this demo searches a small sample dataset. In the integrated app, it searches the full live register (~100,000+ organisations). Always verify at the official source: gov.uk — Register of Licensed Sponsors (Workers)
Who needs this check: Track A applicants (new sponsorship or sponsor change), Track B students planning the move to Skilled Worker after graduation, and Track E/D users targeting sponsored roles. Who does not: if you have unrestricted work rights (Tracks C, D, E post-grant, F, and settled status) you can apply anywhere — mark those applications "sponsorship not needed" in the pipeline below.
Where to find sponsorship-friendly jobs
The register itself — as a target list
Download the register CSV, filter to your city and sector, and you have a list of every employer in your area that CAN sponsor. Approach them directly — most immigrants never think of this.
gov.uk — search "register of licensed sponsors workers"
UKHired
Job board listing only visa-sponsoring roles, filterable by salary threshold and occupation eligibility.
ukhired.com
LinkedIn — keyword technique
Search your role + "visa sponsorship" OR "Skilled Worker visa" in quotes. Also set job alerts with these keywords — sponsored roles say so more often than people expect.
linkedin.com/jobs
NHS Jobs (health professionals)
The NHS is the UK's largest sponsor. Trusts state sponsorship availability on listings; most clinical roles at Band 3+ are eligible occupations.
jobs.nhs.uk
