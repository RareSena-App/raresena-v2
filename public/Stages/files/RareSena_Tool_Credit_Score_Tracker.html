What moves the line — monthly actions
If the line is flat, check these in order
1.Electoral roll registered (if eligible) — the single fastest lift; appears within 4–6 weeks
2.Credit-builder card paid in full by direct debit — zero missed payments, usage under 30% of limit
3.Rent reporting active (CreditLadder or Canopy) — your largest payment made visible
4.At least two direct debits running from your main account
5.No new credit applications in the last 3 months — hard searches suppress scores temporarily
6.Errors disputed — wrong addresses and unknown accounts drag scores; check all three reports quarterly
🔧 Developer integration: Persist to Supabase (suggested table: credit_scores — user_id, month, experian, equifax, transunion). Monthly push notification: "Time to log your credit scores" on the user's chosen day. Trend chart on dashboard. Note: scores are user-entered — no Open Banking or credit API integration required for v1.
