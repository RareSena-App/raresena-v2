Part 1 — Your daily numbers & macros
Plain terms, once: calories (kcal) = the energy in food. Macros = the three building blocks every food is made of — protein (repairs and builds your body), carbs (carbohydrates — your fuel), and fat (hormones, warmth, flavour). g simply means grams — the weight of each one you need in a day.

Two things worth knowing: ① Everyone in the UK can get free dietitian support — ask your GP for a referral; you do not need to manage a condition's diet alone. ② For women: period pain that disrupts your life, very heavy bleeding, or symptoms you've been told to "just live with" deserve a GP appointment. Conditions like endometriosis take years to diagnose partly because people are told their symptoms are normal. They are common — and they are treatable.
